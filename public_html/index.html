About Me

My name is Christina Appadoo. Becoming a model has always been a dream of mine. I am very passionate about diversity and representation in all aspects of life. I hope to pave the way for other young black women and women of color to be happy and confident in themselves. Modeling also offers the chance to travel and experience new cultures which is something I am very passionate about. I hope to gain the opportunity to model internationally and take advantage of that opportunity to submerge myself in different cultures.
